Misty

An extremely lively Dragon Spirit, it rushes on the mountainside of Tranquil Mountain all day long, exuding bursts fluctuating Spirit Field. It is one of the famous Tranquil Mountain's scenic spots, but if you happen to crash into its aperture, you need to watch out for its pranks.
